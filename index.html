What is Pickleball Pals Society?

Pickleball Pals Society is a non-profit organization that is passionately committed to advancing the sport of Pickleball in the vibrant community of Greater Vancouver.

As ambassadors for the sport, our mission is to cater to players of all ages, skill levels, and abilities. We host a number of different events throughout the year, ranging from casual to competitive. We offer indoor/outdoor recreational play, DUPR rating sessions, learn to play clinics, and tournaments.
