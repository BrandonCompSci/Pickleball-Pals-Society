Pickleball Pals Society is a non-profit organization incorporated under the Societies Act of British Columbia. Our Society is passionately committed to advancing the sport of Pickleball in the vibrant community of Greater Vancouver.

What We Do

As ambassadors for the sport, our mission is to cater to players of all ages, skill levels, and abilities. We host a number of different events throughout the year, ranging from casual to competitive. Our current calendar includes indoor/outdoor recreational play and DUPR rating sessions. In the future, we intend to host tournaments and learn-to-play clinics.
